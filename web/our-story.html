Our Story

Welcome to Bushtec Safari

Bushtec Safari is part of the Canvas and Tent group, the world leader in the manufacture of canvas luxury tents, camping tents and accessories. Established over fifty years ago, Canvas and Tent has a wide range of customers including the military, humanitarian organizations, safari destinations, resorts and individuals around the world. The Bushtec Safari brand has established itself as the elite brand of luxury tents.

Located in Ladysmith, at the foothills of South Africa's Drakensberg mountains, our factory provides over 600 jobs for the local population. The state of the art, award winning factory recently implemented the Eton System to not only revolutionize the manufacturing of our canvas products, but also to remain at the forefront of excellence.

Canvas and Tent has created and implemented an Integrated Quality & Environmental Policy to ensure that at all levels of the business we adhere to exceptional standards. We are committed to producing the highest quality products and at the same time protecting our environment by:


  • 1

    Satisfied Customers

    Satisfying applicable customer and regulatory requirements

  • 2

    Identifying objectives

    Identifying objectives relevant to both quality and environmental management

  • 3

    Determining Controls

    Determining controls that will prevent significant impact of our activities on the environment resulting in pollution, and meet our commitment to protect and enhance the environment.

The quality policy is communicated to all employees at all levels throughout our organization via training, regular internal communications and reinforcement during annual employee performance reviews. The policy is place on the Shared network (Z) for all employees to access and read. Employee understanding of our policies and objectives is determined during internal audits and other methods deemed appropriate.
